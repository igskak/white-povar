Universal Bolognese Sauce

Indulge in the rich and savory flavors of this classic Italian Bolognese sauce, perfect with every bite.

Ingredients:
- 500g ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 200ml red wine
- 200ml milk
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
2. Add diced onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened.
3. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
5. Pour in red wine and let it reduce.
6. Add canned tomatoes and simmer for 1 hour.
7. Stir in milk and simmer for another 30 minutes.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 90 minutes
Servings: 6
Difficulty: Medium
Cuisine: Italian
Category: Sauce
Tags: italian, sauce, pasta, meat, traditional
