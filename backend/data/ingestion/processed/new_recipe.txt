Delicious Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, and pancetta.

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 200g pancetta, diced
- 4 large eggs
- 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Black pepper to taste
- Salt for pasta water

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package directions.
2. While pasta cooks, heat a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Add pancetta and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
4. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute more.
5. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated cheese.
6. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
7. Add hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta.
8. Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed.
9. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Medium
Cuisine: Italian
Category: Main Course
Tags: pasta, italian, eggs, cheese
