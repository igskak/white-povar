Shakshuka

A vibrant and flavorful Middle Eastern dish featuring eggs poached in a rich, spiced tomato sauce.

Ingredients:
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 400g canned tomatoes
- Cumin to taste
- Dried coriander to taste
- Paprika to taste
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Sugar to taste
- Olive oil for cooking
- 6 fresh eggs
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
- Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large cast-iron skillet over medium heat.
2. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
3. Add minced garlic and chopped bell pepper, cook for 3 minutes.
4. Add cumin, coriander, and paprika, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
5. Add canned tomatoes, salt, pepper, and sugar. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
6. Create small wells in the sauce and crack eggs into each well.
7. Cover and cook for 8-12 minutes until egg whites are set.
8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and parsley before serving.

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 3
Difficulty: Medium
Cuisine: Middle Eastern
Category: Main Course
Tags: eggs, tomatoes, spiced, vegetarian, brunch
