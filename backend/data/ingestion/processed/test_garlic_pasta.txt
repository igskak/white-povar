Simple Garlic Pasta

A quick and delicious pasta dish perfect for weeknight dinners.

Ingredients:
- 300g spaghetti
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 60ml olive oil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package directions.
2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
3. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Drain pasta, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
5. Add pasta to the pan with garlic oil.
6. Toss with Parmesan cheese and parsley.
7. Add pasta water if needed to create a silky sauce.
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 12 minutes
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Easy
Cuisine: Italian
Category: Main Course
Tags: pasta, quick, vegetarian, garlic
