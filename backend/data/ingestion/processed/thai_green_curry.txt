Thai Green Curry with Chicken

An authentic Thai curry with aromatic green curry paste, coconut milk, and tender chicken.

Ingredients:
- 500g chicken thigh, sliced
- 400ml coconut milk
- 3 tbsp green curry paste
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp palm sugar
- 4 Thai eggplants, quartered
- 100g green beans, trimmed
- 2 kaffir lime leaves
- 1 red chili, sliced
- Thai basil leaves
- Jasmine rice for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat thick coconut milk in a wok until oil separates.
2. Add green curry paste and fry until fragrant.
3. Add chicken and cook until nearly done.
4. Add remaining coconut milk and bring to boil.
5. Add fish sauce, palm sugar, eggplants, and green beans.
6. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
7. Add lime leaves and chili.
8. Garnish with Thai basil and serve with jasmine rice.

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Medium
Cuisine: Thai
Category: Main Course
Tags: curry, thai, coconut, spicy, chicken
