Chicken Tikka Masala

A creamy and aromatic Indian curry with tender marinated chicken in a rich tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients:
- 500g chicken breast, cubed
- 200ml Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- Salt to taste
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp paprika
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 200ml heavy cream
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
- Basmati rice for serving

Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, turmeric, garlic, ginger, and salt for 2 hours.
2. Heat oil in a large pan and cook marinated chicken until golden.
3. Remove chicken and set aside.
4. In the same pan, saut√© onion until golden.
5. Add garlic, ginger, and spices, cook for 1 minute.
6. Add tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
7. Blend the sauce until smooth.
8. Return sauce to pan, add cream and chicken.
9. Simmer for 10 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
10. Garnish with cilantro and serve with basmati rice.

Prep Time: 30 minutes (plus 2 hours marinating)
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Medium
Cuisine: Indian
Category: Main Course
Tags: curry, chicken, creamy, spiced, indian
