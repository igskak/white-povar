Classic Margherita Pizza

A traditional Italian pizza with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.

Ingredients:
- 300g pizza dough
- 200g San Marzano tomatoes, crushed
- 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
- 20 fresh basil leaves
- 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- Black pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F).
2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
3. Spread crushed tomatoes evenly over the dough.
4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
7. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
8. Serve immediately while hot.

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 12 minutes
Servings: 2
Difficulty: Easy
Cuisine: Italian
Category: Main Course
Tags: pizza, italian, vegetarian, traditional
